How to Access your IEE eLibrary

Overview

EIT subscribes to the IEEE Xplore 'STEM 45 PLUS' Journals Collection which provides students with access to:

  • 45 IEEE periodicals plus IEEE Spectrum.
  • Unlimited full-text access to each title via IEEE Xplore with PDF and HTML version available.
  • Highly cited and peer-reviewed titles to ensure content is both high quality and relevant.
  • Specially selected content across numerous technology areas.
  • Access to the complete backfile of each title via IEEE Xplore.
