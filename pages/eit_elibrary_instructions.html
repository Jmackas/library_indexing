How to Access your EIT eLibrary

Logging in

As an EIT Student, you have access to all 160+ technical manuals in the EIT eLibrary for the duration of your EIT program. To access the full eLibrary, please go to this link.

There are two primary methods of accessing your eBook:

  • Online viewing
  • Offline viewing

Log in with the following details via the button on the top right corner:

Username: AdvS
Password: bmal117

Accessing eBooks Online

  1. To view eBooks through your browser, click on the tab 'eBooks Online'. You will then see a full collection of eBooks viewable through your browser via Windows, Macintosh or iOS devices.
  2. Scroll down to the book you want, and select 'View Online'.
  3. There are various tools available to help you interact with the eBook.

Accessing eBooks Offline

  1. The external software CopySafe Reader is required to view the eBooks, and will need to be installed.
  2. Go to the tab 'Masters Library' on the toolbar. You will see the full list of eBooks including the eBook name, eBook code and version number.
  3. Scroll down to the book you want to download, and select the link.
  4. Open CopySafe, and select the downloaded eBook.
